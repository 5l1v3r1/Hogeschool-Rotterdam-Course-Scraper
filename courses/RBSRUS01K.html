Cursusdoelen
Students learn to read and write in Russian, using the Cyrillic alphabet. They acquire understanding of and learn to apply the following grammatical notions: the verb ‘to be’ in the present tense, the gender of nouns, some possessive pronouns, personal pronouns, imperative, some verb forms in the present tense, numerals till 10, the genitive form of feminine and masculine nouns, the use of the genitive case in Russian, impersonal constructions.
Students acquire understanding of and learn to apply vocabulary with regard to the following topics: the airport, the street, the family, introducing yourself. Students learn to recognize and use Russian name and forms of address, acquire information about some Russian cities and their airports.
 
 
Inhoud
Beginniveau: pure beginner.

Werkvormen: Hoorcolleges + werkcolleges.

Inhoud: This course introduces students to the basics of the Russian language. It is taught on the basis of Ruslan Russian 1 text- and work book. Introduction and the first three chapters are dealt with: the airport, the street, the family.

Toetsvorm: Written summative at the end of the course.
