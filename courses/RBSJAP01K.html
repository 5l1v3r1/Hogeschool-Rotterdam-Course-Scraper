Cursusdoelen
In line with the CEFR A1 (minus)
  • Can recognize familiar words and very basic phrases concerning oneself and family in immediate and concrete surroundings when people speak slowly and clearly. (Understanding Listening)
  • Can understand familiar names, words and very simple sentences. (Understanding Reading)
  • Can interview for each other with simple questions and answers, delivered directly to him/her in clear, slow and repeated speech by a sympathetic speaker (Speaking Spoken Interaction)
  • Can ask and answer simple questions in areas of simple personal information. (Speaking Spoken Interaction)
  • Can write and read simple words and sentences. (Writing)
 
Inhoud
Startlevel: Student can follow the course without any knowledge of Japanese
Japanese for beginners is a course for students who do not have any knowledge of Japanese. The course books are: Genki I second edition, an integrated course in elementary Japanese and its workbook. The following chapters will be dealt with: Lesson 1 – New Friends, Lesson 2 – Shopping and Reading and Writing section.
Students acquire understanding of and learn to apply the following grammatical notions: A is B and A is not B. Yes- and No questions and open questions in the present tense, numerals until 100000, times and ages, self introduction and interview others and tell about your close family. Students acquire understanding of and learn to communicate orally in Japanese with regard to the following topics: self introduction, ask some personal questions, order some foods and/or drinks in a restaurant, and do shopping.

Workform: Interactieve werkcolleges met spreekoefeningen, schrijf en leesoefeningen.

Content: Introduction, Lessons 1 and 2 from the text book and the work book

Examform: Written summative at the end of the course.
Written and oral formative during the lessons. A random number of above listed MLOs will be tested.

Literature: Marugoto A1 Katsudo
ISBN:  9783875487077
Reader:  https://www.studiebijdehand.nl/p/2010000012130/reader-keuzevak-japans-marugoto-japanese-language-and-culture
Studenten dienen deze lesmaterialen vanaf de eerste les bij zich te hebben.

Note: Students may, for whatever reason, miss a maximum of 2 lectures to be admitted to the test. This is a real basic course in Japanese in which one can get acquainted with the structure of a language that is totally different from the European languages.
